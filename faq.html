Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about orders, delivery, returns, and more.

Where is my order?

Most orders arrive within 2–5 working days. If your order is delayed, please contact us with your order number and we’ll check the status for you.

How do I return an item?

You can return unused items within 14 days of delivery. Items must be in original packaging and in resellable condition. Visit our Returns Policy for full details.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes. Once we receive and inspect your returned item, we issue a refund to your original payment method.

Can I change my delivery address?

If your order has not yet been dispatched, we can update your delivery address. Contact us as soon as possible with your order number.

Do you ship outside the UK?

At the moment, we only ship within the United Kingdom.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit/debit cards, PayPal, and digital wallet payments.
