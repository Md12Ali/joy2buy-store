Store Policies

Clear and simple policies designed to give you confidence when shopping with us.

14‑Day Return Policy

We want you to be happy with your purchase. If something isn’t right, you may return most items within 14 days of delivery.

Return Conditions

  • Items must be unused and in their original packaging.
  • Items must be in resellable condition.
  • Returns must include proof of purchase (order number or confirmation email).

Refunds

Refunds are issued to your original payment method once the returned item has been inspected.

Non‑Returnable Items

For hygiene reasons, used bedding or damaged items cannot be returned.

Shipping & Delivery

Delivery Times

  • Standard UK delivery: 2–5 working days.
  • Orders are usually dispatched within 1–2 working days.
  • Tracking information is provided when available.

Shipping Fees

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your location and order size.

Privacy Notice

Your privacy matters to us. We only collect information necessary to process your order and provide customer support.

Information We Collect

  • Name and contact details
  • Shipping address
  • Order history
  • Payment confirmation (processed securely by third‑party providers)

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your order
  • To provide customer support
  • To improve our store and services

We do not sell or share your personal information with external companies for marketing purposes.

Terms & Conditions

Use of Our Website

By using this website, you agree to shop responsibly and provide accurate information during checkout.

Product Information

We aim to provide accurate product descriptions. Colours may vary slightly due to screen settings.

Pricing

Prices are displayed in GBP and may change without notice.

Order Acceptance

Your order is confirmed once payment is successfully processed.
